about me

i believe there are 2 types of designers in this world — the ones who work as a designer and the ones who live as a designer.

i’m the latter!

If you were to visit me right now, you'll find me tinkering away with something — making things with pixels, putting pen to paper, bringing ideas to life. I possess an undying passion for product, typography and graphic design.

I’m fascinated by tech and how well-designed products help elevate our everyday experiences. I game, read about digital products/ design/ productivity and learn new things on YouTube in my free time.

When it comes to work, I'm always mindful of the micro-narrative and I constantly strive to take one step closer towards the big picture.

Above all, I listen with intention and I execute with sheer passion.
